What we believe in?

Global challenges today need to be addressed by a diversity of stakeholders, and anyone can and should be able to collaborate. Young people around the world are doing amazing things already, transforming their realities for the better. With the right support and empowerment (and breaking limiting structures), every young person can become a global shaker. Population under 30 represent half of the world's population. If we are taking long term decisions or thinking solutions, young people need to be at the decision making table. We are more than nationalities, we are a generation. The only winner during our discussions and interactions will be YOUth.

About the SIW

The Social Innovation Warehouse is a international resource bank for communities, local governments and individuals to find replicable and adaptable projects that can become solutions for the challenges they are facing. Do you want to bring agility to the classroom or work on XXI century skills? Maybe tackle the digital gap or innovate in how you approach youth employment issues? Is your country trying to find new ways to incorporate technology in rural areas or try to tackle social gender equality matters? Search the platform and navigate through the projects that are already in motion around the world to find the one that suits you best and would help you face the issue on hand.

About Y20

The Youth 20 (or Y20) is the youth engagement group for the G2O, the leading forum of the world's 20 major economies. As the G2O does, each year the Y20 organizes a Summit that reunites youth from all over the G2O territories and guest countries, to debate and work on recommendation that reflect the youth point of views regarding the main topics and priorities chosen by the Presidency.
